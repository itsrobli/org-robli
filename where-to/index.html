Where To

find places to go

About

You're with a group of friends and nobody can make a decision about where to go next. There are too many choices and nobody wants to make the decision for everyone else.

Can everyone at least agree on a general place to go? Drinks? Coffee? Ice cream? Restaurant?

And at the end of the night, if nobody liked the place, you can all blame the app.

If this is not what you want in an app, you can just flick around the colourful buttons with your thumb instead － that's fun too. Otherwise, I recommend using Google Maps, Urbanspoon or Yelp for something full featured; or Foodspotting or Posse for something a little different. All of these apps are available on the App Store.

Version 1.0

This app picks for you an ice cream or gelato place that is within walking distance of your current location.

It allows you a small amount of control by letting you choose one of four options:

  • Popular: the most popular place nearby.
  • Highest rated: the nearest place with the highest review rating.
  • Open now: a nearby place that is known to be open right now.
  • Closest: the absolutely closest place with no regard to anything else.

My hope is that if you are with a group of friends, and nobody can make a decision about where to go for dessert, this app will help break that deadlock. It's like a Magic 8-Ball － but instead of helping make important life decisions it just tells you where to find ice cream and gelato.

If this is not what you want in an app, you can just flick around the colourful buttons with your thumb instead － that's fun too.
